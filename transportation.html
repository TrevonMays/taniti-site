Getting To & Around Taniti

Arriving by Air

Small airport for jets and prop planes; expansion planned for larger jets.

Arriving by Ship

Small cruise ship docks once per week in Yellow Leaf Bay.

Public Buses

Buses in Taniti City run 5 a.m. to 11 p.m. daily.

Rental Cars & Taxis

Local rental agency near the airport and taxis in Taniti City.

Bikes & Walking

Bikes with helmets available to rent; city is flat and walkable.
